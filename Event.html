OUR PAST EVENT

Waste No More Campaign

Date: September 15, 2024

Venue: Sunway College

Event Description:
We held an awareness drive to educate the public on how to reduce food waste at home. We provide practical tips for minimizing waste, understanding food expiration labels, and using surplus food creatively.

Highlights:

  • Over 300 attendess participated in the event.
  • 30 volunteers helped organize the event.
  • Interactive workshops on how to repurpose leftovers into meals.

UPCOMING EVENT

Foodbank Volunteering

Date: February 15, 2025

Time: 9.00AM - 3.00PM

Venue: Food Bank Malaysia HQ, Jalan Jelapang, Kuala Lumpur

Event Description:
Join us in combating hunger and reduce food waste in local communities. This event brings together volunteers to help with food packaging, sorting, and distribution to families in need.

Highlights:

  • Collaborating with Food Bank Malaysia.
  • Assist in sorting surplus food donated by different locations (supermarket, restaurant, manufacturer).
  • Help deliver food to charity and families in low-income communities.

"Volunteering isn’t just about giving food, it’s about giving dignity and care."

Cooking Class - Using Surplus Food

Date: March 5, 2025

Time: 10.00AM - 12.00PM

Venue: Panda Pagoda, Kuala Lumpur

Event Description:
Learn to cook delicious meals using surplus or 'ugly' vegetables that would otherwise go to waste. Join us for an interactive class where our chef will guide you through making sustainable meals.

  • Hands-on cooking session

  • Q&A session
